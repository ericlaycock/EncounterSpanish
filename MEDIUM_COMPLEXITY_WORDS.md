# MEDIUM Complexity Words - 1000 Most Common Spanish Words

Words that need notes to avoid confusion but don't require learning entire grammar principles.

## Articles & Gender (MEDIUM)
- **el** (rank 6) - "the (masculine)" - Needs note about gender agreement
- **la** (rank 5) - "the (feminine)" - Needs note about gender agreement  
- **un** (rank 11) - "a, an (masculine)" - Needs note about gender
- **una** (rank 15) - "a, an (feminine)" - Needs note about gender
- **los** (rank 17) - "the (masculine plural)" - Gender + plural
- **las** (rank 28) - "the (feminine plural)" - Gender + plural
- **unos** (rank 255) - "some (masculine plural)" - Gender + plural
- **unas** (rank 434) - "some (feminine plural)" - Gender + plural

## Accent Marks Change Meaning (MEDIUM)
- **qué** (rank 13) - "what" vs **que** (rank 1) - "that, which" - Accent changes meaning
- **cuándo** (rank 428) - "when (question)" vs **cuando** (rank 52) - "when (conjunction)"
- **dónde** (rank 90) - "where (question)" vs **donde** (rank 152) - "where (relative)"
- **cuál** (rank 347) - "which (question)" vs **cual** (rank 539) - "which (relative)"
- **cuánto** (rank 327) - "how much (question)" vs **cuanto** (rank 534) - "as much as"
- **cuántos** (rank 954) - "how many (question)"
- **adónde** (rank 532) - "where to (question)"
- **porqué** (rank 912) - "why (noun)" vs **porque** (rank 86) - "because"

## Yes/No Confusion (MEDIUM)
- **sí** (rank 29, 74) - "yes" vs **si** (rank 23) - "if" - Accent matters!

## Multiple Meanings/Context-Dependent (MEDIUM)
- **hay** (rank 44) - "there is/are" (present) - Also used for past: "había" (rank 202)
- **ser** (rank 69) vs **estar** (rank 136) - Both mean "to be" but different uses
- **a** (rank 4) - "to, at" - Personal "a" rule (a + person)
- **de** (rank 2) - "of, from" - Possession, origin, material
- **por** (rank 12) vs **para** (rank 20) - Both mean "for" but different uses
- **más** (rank 37) - "more" vs **mas** (rank 233) - "but (formal)" - Accent matters!

## Contractions (MEDIUM)
- **del** (rank 33) - "of the" (de + el) - Only contraction in Spanish
- **al** (rank 34) - "to the" (a + el) - Only contraction in Spanish

## Pronouns - Indirect Object (MEDIUM)
- **le** (rank 36) - "him, her, you (formal)" - Indirect object pronoun
- **les** (rank 151) - "them, you (plural)" - Indirect object pronoun
- **se** (rank 18) - "oneself, itself" - Reflexive/impersonal, changes meaning of verbs

## Special Verb Constructions (MEDIUM)
- **gusta** (rank 194) / **gustan** (rank 838) - "like" but backwards: "Me gusta" = "It pleases me"
- **hace falta** - "need" but construction is different
- **tener que** - "have to" (obligation)
- **hay que** - "one must" (impersonal obligation)

## Time Expressions (MEDIUM)
- **hace** (rank 107) - "ago" in time expressions: "hace dos días" = "two days ago"
- **desde** (rank 176) - "since, from" - Time origin
- **hasta** (rank 118) - "until, even" - Time endpoint
- **durante** (rank 397) - "during" - Duration

## Demonstratives - This/That (MEDIUM)
- **este** (rank 54) - "this (masculine)" vs **esta** (rank 41) - "this (feminine)"
- **ese** (rank 80) - "that (masculine)" vs **esa** (rank 94) - "that (feminine)"
- **esto** (rank 38) - "this (neuter)" - Used when noun is unknown
- **eso** (rank 27) - "that (neuter)" - Used when noun is unknown
- **aquel/aquella** - "that (far)" - Three-way distinction: este/ese/aquel

## Possessives - Agreement (MEDIUM)
- **mi** (rank 21) - "my" vs **mis** (rank 134) - "my (plural)"
- **tu** (rank 31) - "your (informal)" vs **tus** (rank 172) - "your (plural)"
- **su** (rank 30) - "his, her, your, their" - Ambiguous! Needs context
- **sus** (rank 85) - "their, your (plural)" - Ambiguous!

## Prepositions - Multiple Uses (MEDIUM)
- **con** (rank 19) - "with" - Also: "conmigo" (rank 209), "contigo" (rank 225)
- **sin** (rank 119) - "without"
- **sobre** (rank 123) - "on, about" - Physical vs abstract
- **entre** (rank 270) - "between, among" - Two vs many
- **contra** (rank 307) - "against"

## Question Words (MEDIUM)
- **quién** (rank 88) - "who" vs **quien** (rank 212) - "who (relative)"
- **cómo** (rank 59) - "how" - Also in greetings: "¿Cómo estás?"

## Special Constructions (MEDIUM)
- **tener** (rank 189) - "to have" - Also: "tener que" = "have to"
- **poder** (rank 318) - "to be able to, can" - Also: "poder" as noun = "power"
- **deber** - "must, should, owe" - Multiple meanings
- **soler** - "usually" - Special construction

## Summary by Category

**Total MEDIUM complexity words: ~50-60 words**

1. **Articles with gender** (8 words): el, la, un, una, los, las, unos, unas
2. **Accent marks** (8 words): qué/que, cuándo/cuando, dónde/donde, cuál/cual, cuánto/cuanto, sí/si, más/mas, porqué/porque
3. **Multiple "to be"** (2 words): ser, estar
4. **Prepositions "for"** (2 words): por, para
5. **Contractions** (2 words): del, al
6. **Indirect object pronouns** (3 words): le, les, se
7. **Special verb constructions** (3+ words): gusta/gustan, tener que, hay que
8. **Time expressions** (4 words): hace, desde, hasta, durante
9. **Demonstratives** (5+ words): este/esta, ese/esa, esto/eso
10. **Possessives** (4 words): mi/mis, tu/tus, su/sus
11. **Question vs relative** (6 words): qué/que, cuándo/cuando, dónde/donde, cuál/cual, quién/quien

These words need notes because:
- They have rules that aren't obvious from English
- They have multiple meanings depending on context
- They look similar to other words (accent marks)
- They work differently than English equivalents (gustar, ser/estar)
